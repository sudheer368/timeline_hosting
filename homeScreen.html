Timeline Social Media - Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Welcome to the Timeline platform, a social media platform where you can share your memories, stories, and experiences with your friends and the world. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your information when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

When you use our Timeline platform, we collect the following types of information to enhance your experience:

  • Authentication Information: When you sign up or log into your account, we collect your login credentials such as your username, password, and any other necessary details to authenticate your identity.
  • Personal Information: We collect personal information like your name, email address, phone number, and profile information to create and manage your account and to allow you to connect with others.
  • Location Information: If you choose to share your location, we collect this information to provide location-based features, such as geotagging your posts and helping you connect with users nearby.
  • Phone Number: We may collect your phone number for authentication and verification purposes, as well as for communication regarding your account.
  • Email Address: We collect your email address for communication purposes, including notifications, updates, and security alerts.
  • Memories (Posts/Media): As a social platform, we collect the memories you share, including images, videos, and text content that you post on your timeline.
  • IP Address: We collect your IP address to monitor usage, improve security, and personalize your experience on the platform.
  • Mobile Device Information: We collect data about your device, including the model, operating system, and version, for performance optimization and ensuring compatibility with the platform.
  • Activity Information: We track your activity on the platform, such as the posts you like, comment on, and share, as well as the people you interact with. This helps personalize your experience and recommendations.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To Provide and Improve Services: We use your information to provide you with the features of the Timeline platform, such as posting, commenting, and sharing memories. Your information helps us personalize your experience and improve the services.
  • To Communicate with You: We may use your email address or phone number to send important notifications, updates about your account, security alerts, and promotional content.
  • To Protect Your Security: We use your information to ensure the security of your account, including detecting fraudulent activities and preventing unauthorized access.
  • To Customize Your Experience: We use your information, including activity data and preferences, to personalize the content, suggestions, and ads you see on the platform.

3. Sharing Your Information

We value your privacy and do not share your personal information with third-party applications or services without your consent. However, the information you share on your timeline, such as posts, media, and other content, will be visible to others within the platform according to your privacy settings. Please be mindful of what you share publicly.

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. While we strive to safeguard your data, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We encourage you to take reasonable precautions to protect your account.

5. Your Privacy Choices

You have control over your information:

  • Access Your Information: You can access and update your personal information at any time through your account settings.
  • Delete Your Content: You can delete the posts, memories, and content you've shared on the platform, but some data may be retained for legal and security purposes.
  • Change Privacy Settings: You can adjust the visibility of your posts and memories to control who can view your content.
  • Opt-Out of Marketing Communications: You can opt out of receiving marketing emails by following the unsubscribe link in the email.
